Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean this yoga mat?

To clean your yoga mat, use a mixture of mild soap and water. Gently wipe the mat with a soft cloth, rinse with clean water, and allow it to air dry completely before rolling it up.

Is this mat suitable for hot yoga?

Yes, this mat is suitable for hot yoga. Its moisture-resistant surface provides excellent grip even in sweaty conditions.

What are the dimensions of the mat?

The standard size of our Premium Yoga Mat is 68" x 24" (172 cm x 61 cm). We also offer a larger size of 72" x 26" (183 cm x 66 cm) for taller practitioners.

Is the mat eco-friendly?

Yes, our Premium Yoga Mat is made from eco-friendly, biodegradable materials. We are committed to reducing environmental impact in our production process.
